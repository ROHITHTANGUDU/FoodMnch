Order Cancellation, Returns, and Policies

Order Cancellation

If you wish to cancel your order, please follow these guidelines:

  • Orders can be canceled within 30 minutes of placing the order.
  • Visit your order history and click on the 'Cancel' button next to the order you want to cancel.
  • Refunds for canceled orders will be processed within 3-5 business days.

Returns

We accept returns for the following reasons:

  • Incorrect items delivered
  • Items damaged during delivery

If you encounter any of these issues, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery.
